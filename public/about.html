About Me

I am an experienced elevator mechanic who has decided to transition into a career in software development, driven by a passion for technology and innovation. My professional journey began with a background as an electronics technician, which laid the foundation for my technical expertise. Over the years, I have worked in various reputable companies, building a strong reputation for troubleshooting, maintenance, and leadership in the elevator industry.

My career highlights include:

    Serving as a Technical Manager at Chuck E. Cheese, overseeing complex technical systems. Working as a Troubleshooting Supervisor at Liftco Elevator, where I specialized in diagnosing and resolving critical elevator issues. Gaining hands-on experience as a Maintenance and Troubleshooting Mechanic at Service Tech Alan Elevator, Dunwell Elevator, Pride and Service, and most recently, Unitech Elevator Company.

In addition to my mechanical and technical experience, I hold a Cybersecurity Certificate from the California Institute of Advanced Technology (CIAT), showcasing my commitment to staying at the forefront of technology. I am now channeling my diverse skills and problem-solving abilities into the world of software development, where I aim to combine my technical background and newfound expertise in coding to create innovative solutions that bridge the gap between technology and industry needs.
